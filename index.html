How It Works

Get Started in Minutes

Whether you are a patient or a healthcare provider, CuraX is designed to be up and running immediately.

For Patients
1
Download the App
Available on iOS and Android. Sign up with your phone number in under 60 seconds.
2
Search
Find clinics, doctors, and diagnostic labs near you.
3
Book & Pay
Confirm your slot instantly and pay securely via UPI, card, or CuraX Wallet.
4
Visit & Get Records
QR check-in at the clinic. Prescriptions and reports stored automatically in your health record.
For Clinic & Hospital Partners
1
Register Your Clinic
Our team handles onboarding. Go live on CuraX within 48 hours of submission.
2
Set Up Services
Add your doctors, services, pricing, and slot availability via the Partner App.
3
Receive Bookings
Appointments arrive in real time. Manage your queue, consultations, and billing from one screen.
4
Grow Your Practice
Access analytics, patient insights, and settlement reports to drive growth month on month.
